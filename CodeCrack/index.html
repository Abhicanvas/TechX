ABOUT CTF

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve a series of challenges to capture "flags" – pieces of data hidden within the system. These competitions test your skills in various domains of cybersecurity, including cryptography, web security, reverse engineering, forensics, and more. CTFs are designed to challenge your ability to think critically, adapt under pressure, and apply real-world cybersecurity concepts to solve problems.

ABOUT CodeCrack

CodeCrack is an electrifying Capture The Flag (CTF) competition designed to push your cybersecurity skills to the limit through engaging and diverse decipher challenges. Dive into an immersive 12-hour experience, starting on July 12th that demands critical thinking, problem-solving, and the ability to decipher challenges to capture the flag under pressure. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned expert, CodeCrack provides a thrilling platform to test your prowess in the cybersecurity domains.

Guidelines

Essential Rules and Conduct

Competition Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to the challenge. To ensure a fair, engaging, and secure experience for all participants, please adhere to the following rules:

Scoring System

Points are awarded based on the difficulty level of each challenge and the number of teams that successfully solve them. More difficult challenges and fewer solves result in higher points

Play Fair

Sharing solutions between teams is strictly prohibited. Teams are expected to compete independently and ethically.

Respect the Environment

Any attempt to attack other teams’ systems or the event infrastructure is not allowed. Maintain a respectful and secure environment throughout the event.

Act Responsibly

Disruptive actions that impact the availability, fairness, or integrity of the competition are not tolerated.

Stay Legal

All tools, techniques, and methods used during the competition must comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Teamwork is Key

Collaboration within your team is strongly encouraged. However, collusion or cooperation between different teams is strictly prohibited.

Judges Have the Final Say

All decisions made by the judging panel are final. Judges are responsible for ensuring a fair and balanced competition.

Respect and Integrity

Participants are expected to follow the event’s Code of Conduct and behave with integrity and professionalism at all times.

Victory Awaits

The team with the highest score at the end of the event will be declared the winner.
