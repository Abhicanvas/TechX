ABOUT Event

ABOUT TechX

TechX is an initiative by the Computer Society Student and Young Professionals (SYP), aimed at empowering and grooming future leaders through comprehensive sessions on emerging technologies, soft skills, leadership, and industry engagement. Their goal is to provide a holistic platform to nurture talent and foster professional growth. Funding opportunities of up to 1500 US Dollars available to eligible proposals.

ABOUT CS SYP

IEEE CS SYP is a engaging initiative by the IEEE Computer Society that brings together students and young professionals from across the globe. The IEEE Computer Society SYP TechX initiative is a program designed to empower and groom future leaders in computer science and engineering. It offers funding for innovative projects and sessions on emerging technologies, soft skills, leadership, and industry engagement.

ABOUT IEEE FISAT SB

The IEEE FISAT Student Branch, established in 2003, is among the top five in Kerala, with 460+ members and 100+ new additions yearly. We promote tech innovation, professional growth, and community engagement through events like DROID, Spectrospect, SIGNIFY, IMPULSE, WIEve Epilogue, and RIDE.

ABOUT IEEE CS SBC FISAT

Our Key Initiatives

Advance Skills Through Practical Exposure

CodeCraft

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a thrilling coding competition where participants tackle a range of challenges to earn points. It tests logical thinking, problem-solving, and technical skills, offering a dynamic platform for coders to compete, learn, and showcase their talent under pressure.
Industrial Visit
The Industrial Visit offers students a unique opportunity to observe industry operations firsthand. It helps bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications, providing valuable insights into modern technologies, workflows, and professional practices. This experience enhances students' understanding and prepares them for future roles in the tech-driven industry.
Nanomentoring
Nanomentoring delivers concise, targeted mentoring sessions designed to provide immediate, actionable career guidance in just a few minutes. Through one-on-one interactions with industry experts, participants can clarify doubts, explore opportunities in specific domains, and gain critical insights into current industry trends and essential skills, empowering them to confidently shape their future careers with expert support.
