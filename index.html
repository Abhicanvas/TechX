ABOUT Event

TechXQuintessence is a flagship initiative by IEEE CS SBC FISAT in collaboration with IEEE CS SYP TechX Initiative and IEEE CS Kerala Chapter, expecting participation from tech enthusiasts. This two-day event combines the power of learning, networking, and competitive spirit through hands-on workshops, expert-led tech talks and an exciting CTF Competition. With over 120 participants

ABOUT IEEE FISAT SB

The IEEE FISAT Student Branch, established in 2003, is among the top five in Kerala, with 460+ members and 100+ new additions yearly. We promote tech innovation, professional growth, and community engagement through events like DROID, Spectrospect, SIGNIFY, IMPULSE, WIEve Epilogue, and RIDE.

ABOUT IEEE CS SBC FISAT

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter at FISAT is a vibrant center for innovation, skill-building, and impactful initiatives. Consistently recognized for excellence, it secured 2nd place in 2021 and 3rd place in both 2022 and 2023 in the CS SBC Chapter Presentations

ABOUT TechX

TechX is an initiative by the Computer Society Student and Young Professionals (SYP), aimed at empowering and grooming future leaders through comprehensive sessions on emerging technologies, soft skills, leadership, and industry engagement. Their goal is to provide a holistic platform to nurture talent and foster professional growth. Funding opportunities of up to 1500 US Dollars available to eligible proposals.
